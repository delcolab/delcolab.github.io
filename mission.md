# Mission & Values

## Mission
To understand how mitochondrial biology and mechanical forces shape joint health across species and to fuel innovation in orthoregenerative medicine.

## Scientific Values
- Comparative insight  
- Mechanistic depth  
- Translational impact

## Lab Culture

- Rigor
